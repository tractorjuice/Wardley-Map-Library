## Map Overview

This Wardley Map illustrates the KPI measurement ecosystem for different levels of the Value Pyramid, including Functional, Emotional, Life-Changing, and Social Impact Value. It shows various metrics and components involved in measuring and optimizing the effectiveness of each level.

**Anchor:** The Value Pyramid serves as the anchor, representing the core user need for structured value proposition measurement and optimization.

## Component Analysis

### Value Pyramid

- **Position:** High visibility, high evolution
- **Evolution Stage:** Product (+rental)
- **Strategic Significance:** Central to the entire ecosystem, guiding all measurement efforts

### Functional Value

- **Position:** High visibility, high evolution
- **Evolution Stage:** Product (+rental)
- **Strategic Significance:** Fundamental level of value, crucial for basic product performance

### Emotional Value

- **Position:** High visibility, medium-high evolution
- **Evolution Stage:** Product (+rental)
- **Strategic Significance:** Key differentiator in competitive markets, driving customer loyalty

### Life-Changing Value

- **Position:** High visibility, medium evolution
- **Evolution Stage:** Custom-built
- **Strategic Significance:** Potential for significant competitive advantage and customer retention

### Social Impact Value

- **Position:** High visibility, low-medium evolution
- **Evolution Stage:** Custom-built
- **Strategic Significance:** Emerging area for differentiation and brand building

## Overall Assessment

This Wardley Map represents a sophisticated approach to value proposition measurement, encompassing all levels of the Value Pyramid. The strategic opportunity lies in developing and integrating advanced metrics for life-changing and social impact value, which are currently less evolved.
